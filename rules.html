Bejeweled

Rules

The Game

Bejeweld is an individual / multiplayer game, in which you need to align the 9 different type of jewels.

There are 7 normal jewels, and two special jewels.

The bomb jewel blows up its surrouding area in a 3x3 area.

You must choose the type of game (Infinite or Normal) and it's difficulty

Singleplayer

  • You may move the jewels horizontally and vertically;
  • To earn points, 3 jewels must be aligned horizontally. Each combination of 3 jewels is worth 1 point. (note: the points calculation is done by the equation: (n-3)+1 );
  • To win you must, in the time and the plays given, earn 20 or more points;
  • If you fail to earn the minimum 20 points in the given time or if there are no more possible plays, you will LOSE.

Multiplayer

  • Same rules as the classic game;
  • Each player as 15 seconds to make a play;
  • It's a pass-on game, meaning each player has it's turn on the game.
  • Whoever as more points at the end wins :) .
